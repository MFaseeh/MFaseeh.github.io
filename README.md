## About

I am a PhD Candidate in the Department of Electronic Engineering at **Jeju National University**, South Korea, supervised by **Prof. Do Hyeun Kim** and supported by the **Brain Korea (BK21)** fully-funded scholarship.

My research sits at the intersection of deep learning and applied sciences — building AI-driven solutions for real-world problems across three domains:

- **Computer Vision** — object detection, monocular depth estimation, long-range tracking
- **Natural Language Processing** — text similarity, sarcasm detection, automated essay scoring
- **Material Informatics** — inverse learning and property prediction for ferroelectric ceramics (BaTiO₃)

Prior to my PhD, I spent **6+ years** in academia as a lecturer, lab engineer, and vice principal across institutions in Pakistan.
