About

I am a PhD Candidate in the Department of Electronic Engineering at Jeju National University, South Korea, working under the supervision of Prof. Do Hyeun Kim, supported by the Brain Korea (BK21) fully-funded scholarship.

My research sits at the intersection of deep learning and applied sciences — developing AI-driven methods for computer vision tasks like object detection and depth estimation, NLP applications, and inverse learning frameworks for ceramic material design and properties prediction.

With 23 published and accepted papers across IEEE, Elsevier, Springer, Wiley, MDPI, Taylor & Francis, and PLOS ONE, I combine rigorous research with a strong foundation from 6+ years in academia as a lecturer, lab engineer, and vice principal in Pakistan.
